Coaching Dashboard

1. Opportunity Identified

Effective coaching is essential for improving agent performance in the contact center. However, traditional coaching methods face several challenges:

  • Lack of centralized tracking for coaching sessions and performance improvements.
  • Difficulties in measuring the impact of coaching on key metrics such as quality scores and customer satisfaction.
  • Limited visibility into coaching trends, agent progress, and recurring issues.

2. Proposed Solution

The Coaching Dashboard provides a structured approach to track, analyze, and optimize coaching efforts. Key features include:

  • Centralized Coaching Records: Logs all coaching sessions, feedback, and follow-ups in one place.
  • Performance Tracking: Measures improvements in agent KPIs post-coaching.
  • Trend Analysis: Identifies common coaching themes and agent skill gaps.
  • Automated Insights: Uses Power BI to visualize coaching effectiveness over time.

3. Observed Benefits

By implementing the Coaching Dashboard, we have observed several key benefits:

  • Improved Coaching Efficiency: Structured documentation and follow-ups enhance coaching effectiveness.
  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Insights help prioritize coaching areas for maximum impact.
  • Enhanced Agent Performance: Clear tracking of improvements leads to higher quality scores and customer satisfaction.
  • Proactive Issue Resolution: Identifies recurring challenges and addresses them through targeted coaching.
